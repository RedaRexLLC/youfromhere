📺 Mirror to TV

Pick your device below. Once mirroring, the TV shows the game — the moderator keeps the phone/tablet to control it.

🍎 iPhone or iPad (AirPlay)

1. Connect iPhone/iPad and Apple TV (or AirPlay-compatible TV) to the same Wi-Fi.
2. Swipe down from top-right corner to open Control Center.
3. Tap Screen Mirroring → select your TV.

💻 Mac (AirPlay)

1. Click the Control Center icon in the menu bar (top-right).
2. Click Screen Mirroring → select your Apple TV or AirPlay TV.
3. Or use the AirPlay icon directly in the menu bar if visible.

🤖 Android Phone or Tablet

1. Connect to the same Wi-Fi as your TV.
2. Swipe down for Quick Settings → tap Smart View, Screen Cast, or Cast (name varies by brand).
3. Select your TV or Chromecast device.

🪟 Windows PC

1. Press Windows + K to open the Cast panel.
2. Select your TV or Miracast display.
3. Alternatively use Settings → System → Display → Connect to a wireless display.

🔌 Any Device — HDMI Cable (most reliable)

Plug an HDMI cable from your laptop/phone (with adapter if needed) directly into the TV. Switch the TV input to the correct HDMI port. Works everywhere, no Wi-Fi needed.

💡 Tip: Use Moderator Mode so the answer stays hidden on the TV. The moderator taps "Reveal Answer" on their device when ready.
